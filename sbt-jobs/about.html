About Shama Al Bayan Trading (SBT Services)

Shama Al Bayan Trading (SBT Services) is a fully licensed corporate agency specializing in workforce recruitment, technical staffing, safety auditing, and bulk labor supply within Saudi Arabia. With deep local ties and operations centered in Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam, and NEOM, we serve as the vital bridge linking certified candidates with top contractors.

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver efficient, transparent, and completely legal recruitment services complying with the Saudi Vision 2030 guidelines. We strive to empower candidates with competitive salaries while providing employers with highly-trained, certified professionals.

👁️

Our Vision

To become the absolute benchmark of recruitment safety and engineering supply in the Middle East. We aim to continually modernize our workflows to enable instant, friction-free connections between international talent and local project sites.

Our Core Values

1. Absolute Integrity

We verify all positions, salaries, and client claims. We never list ghost jobs or charge candidates.

2. Safety First

As a certified HSE agency, safety is not just a department — it is our culture and operating standard.

3. Legal Compliance

All contracts, transfers, Iqamas, and health policies strictly match Qiwa and Saudi labor laws.
